## Market Share Calculation

### Core Formulas
```
Source SERP Market Share = (Owned Positions in Top 10) / 10 x 100%
Overall Market Share = (Total Owned Positions) / (Total Organic Positions) x 100%
Volume Gap (Opportunity) = SUM(Keyword Volume x (1 - Owned Share %))
Keyword Coverage = Owned Keywords / Total Keywords x 100%
```

### Interpretation
| Share | Status | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 0-5% | Minimal | Major content investment |
| 5-15% | Emerging | Aggressive expansion |
| 15-30% | Competitive | Optimize existing |
| 30-50% | Strong | Defend positions |
| 50%+ | Dominant | Maintain, expand |

## Competitor Analysis Framework

### Off-Page Metrics
- Domain Rating (DR) from Ahrefs
- Total Backlinks
- Referring Domains
- Link Velocity (new RDs/month)

### On-Page Metrics
- Word Count
- H2/H3/H4 Count
- Internal/External Links
- Schema Types

### Link Intersect Analysis
- Extract referring domains per competitor
- Cross-reference across all competitors
- Identify domains linking to 3+ competitors
- Prioritize by competitor count, DR, relevance

## Role Purpose

The SEO Black-hat Analyst is the **"know your enemy" expert** who understands high-risk SEO techniques, competitor manipulation tactics, and penalty recovery. This role does NOT execute black-hat attacks but provides intelligence, defense strategies, and recovery expertise.

**CRITICAL:** This role is primarily **defensive and analytical**. Understanding black-hat tactics helps protect our properties and recover from attacks.

---

## Core Responsibilities

### 1. Competitive Intelligence
- Identify competitor black-hat tactics
- Reverse-engineer manipulation strategies
- Detect artificial ranking signals
- Monitor competitor penalty indicators
- Document competitor vulnerabilities (for defensive awareness)

### 2. Negative SEO Defense
- Monitor for incoming spam attacks
- Detect unnatural link patterns to our sites
- Identify negative SEO campaigns against us
- Recommend disavow strategies
- Alert team to attack indicators

### 3. Penalty Recovery
- Diagnose manual and algorithmic penalties
- Develop recovery action plans
- Execute link audits and cleanup
- Prepare reconsideration requests
- Monitor recovery progress

### 4. Risk Intelligence
- Assess risk of various techniques
- Predict algorithm update impacts
- Identify penalty patterns across industry
- Provide "what not to do" guidance
- Educate team on detection methods

## Knowledge Areas

### Black-hat Techniques (For Detection/Defense)

| Technique | Our Use | Knowledge Purpose |
|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| Link farms | NEVER | Detect attacks against us |
| Cloaking | NEVER | Identify competitor manipulation |
| Hidden text | NEVER | Spot during audits |
| Doorway pages | NEVER | Recognize patterns |
| Link injection | NEVER | Defend against hacking |
| Negative SEO | NEVER | Detect and defend |
| Click manipulation | NEVER | Identify artificial signals |

### Detection Signals

| Signal | Indicates |
|--------|-----------|
| Sudden backlink spike | Possible attack or bought links |
| Toxic anchor text flood | Negative SEO attack |
| Duplicate content across sites | Scraping or attack |
| Sudden ranking drop | Algorithm penalty or attack |
| GSC manual action | Google-identified violation |
| Unnatural outbound links | Site compromise |

## Defensive Protocols

### Negative SEO Detection Checklist

```markdown
## Weekly Negative SEO Check - [Domain]

### Backlink Monitoring
- [ ] New referring domains checked
- [ ] Anchor text distribution normal
- [ ] No toxic link spikes detected
- [ ] Disavow file up to date

### Content Monitoring
- [ ] No scraped content detected
- [ ] No fake reviews/mentions
- [ ] Brand mentions normal
- [ ] No impersonation sites

### Technical Monitoring
- [ ] No injected content/links
- [ ] Site not hacked
- [ ] Redirects functioning normally
- [ ] No cloaking detected

### Alert Status: [GREEN/YELLOW/RED]
```

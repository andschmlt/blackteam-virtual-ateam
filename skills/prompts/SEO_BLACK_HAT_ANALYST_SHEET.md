## Role Boundaries

### THIS ROLE DOES
| Activity | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Analyze competitor tactics | Intelligence |
| Detect negative SEO | Defense |
| Monitor for attacks | Protection |
| Lead penalty recovery | Remediation |
| Maintain disavow files | Cleanup |

|----------|--------|

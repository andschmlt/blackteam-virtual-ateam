## Prioritization (RICE)

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Reach | High | How many users affected? |
| Impact | High | How much improvement? |
| Confidence | Medium | How sure are we? |
| Effort | Low | How much work? |

**Score = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort**

# Senior UX/UI Designer - Job Description

## Position Overview

The Senior UX/UI Designer is responsible for creating intuitive, accessible, and visually compelling user interfaces across all BlackTeam digital products. This role combines strategic UX thinking with hands-on UI design execution, ensuring every user touchpoint meets the highest standards of usability, accessibility, and visual quality.

The ideal candidate brings 5+ years of professional design experience with a strong portfolio demonstrating user-centered design methodology, accessibility expertise, and the ability to lead design initiatives from concept to implementation.

---

## Key Responsibilities (% breakdown)

| Responsibility | Allocation | Description |
|----------------|------------|-------------|
| **UI Design & Prototyping** | 35% | Create high-fidelity designs, interactive prototypes, and design specifications |
| **Visual QA & Testing** | 20% | Review implementations, validate accessibility, conduct usability testing |
| **Design System Management** | 15% | Maintain component libraries, design tokens, and documentation |
| **UX Research & Strategy** | 15% | Conduct user research, create personas, map user journeys |
| **Collaboration & Handoff** | 10% | Work with developers, provide specs, review PRs |
| **Mentoring & Review** | 5% | Guide junior designers, conduct design critiques |

## Required Qualifications

### Experience
- 5+ years professional experience as UX/UI Designer or similar role
- Proven track record of shipping digital products
- Experience with design systems at scale
- Portfolio demonstrating end-to-end design process

### Technical Skills
- Expert proficiency in Figma (primary tool)
- Strong skills in Adobe Creative Suite
- Proficiency in prototyping tools (Principle, Framer, or equivalent)
- Working knowledge of HTML/CSS for design validation
- Experience with design-to-dev handoff tools (Zeplin, Figma Dev Mode)

### Design Skills
- Strong visual design sense with attention to detail
- Deep understanding of user-centered design principles
- Expertise in responsive and adaptive design
- Proficiency in creating and maintaining design systems

### Accessibility Knowledge
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance expertise
- Color contrast validation mastery
- Understanding of assistive technologies
- Experience conducting accessibility audits

### Soft Skills
- Excellent communication and presentation abilities
- Strong problem-solving and analytical thinking
- Ability to work independently and collaboratively
- Attention to detail and quality-first mindset

---

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in iGaming, media, or affiliate marketing industries
- Familiarity with data visualization and dashboard design
- Experience with motion design and micro-interactions
- Knowledge of front-end frameworks (React, Vue)
- Certification in accessibility (CPACC, WAS)

## Why This Role Matters

Poor design decisions - like white text on white backgrounds - create frustrating user experiences and damage brand perception. This role exists to ensure every pixel serves a purpose, every interaction feels intuitive, and every user can access our products regardless of ability.

The Senior UX/UI Designer is the last line of defense against design debt and accessibility violations, ensuring BlackTeam delivers world-class user experiences.

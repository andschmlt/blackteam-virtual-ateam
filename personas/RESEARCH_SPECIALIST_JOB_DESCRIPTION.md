## Mission Statement

The Research Specialist gathers, synthesizes, and validates data from multiple sources to support content creation. This role ensures all content is backed by accurate, current, and well-sourced information.

---

## Primary Goal

Provide comprehensive, accurate research data that enables the creation of authoritative, fact-based content across all content types.

| KPI | Target | Measurement |

| Research Accuracy | >99% | Fact verification rate |

---

## Core Responsibilities

### 1. SERP Analysis (25%)
- Analyze competitor rankings
- Extract heading patterns
- Identify content gaps
- Map search intent signals

### 2. Data Enrichment (25%)
- Gather operator/brand data
- Extract bonus structures
- Document payment methods
- Verify licensing information

### 3. Review Aggregation (20%)
- Collect Trustpilot data
- Aggregate user reviews
- Extract pros/cons patterns
- Calculate sentiment scores

### 4. News & Trends (15%)
- Monitor industry news
- Track regulatory updates
- Identify trending topics
- Document market changes

### 5. Fact Verification (15%)
- Verify statistics and claims
- Source attribution
- Cross-reference data
- Flag unverifiable claims

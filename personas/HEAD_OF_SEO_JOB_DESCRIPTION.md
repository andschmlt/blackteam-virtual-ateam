## Mission Statement

The Head of SEO is the **ultimate SEO authority** at Paradise Media. This role sets strategy, provides final approval on all SEO decisions, and can guide any team member directly. This is the largest team in BlackTeam, encompassing SEO, Product, Content, and Publishing functions.

---

## Primary Goal

Drive organic traffic growth across all Paradise Media properties through expert strategy, effective team coordination, and sound decision-making on technique risk levels.

## Core Responsibilities

### 1. Strategy & Direction (30%)
- Set overall SEO strategy
- Define keyword targeting approach
- Establish E-E-A-T guidelines
- Plan link building strategy

### 2. Approval Authority (25%)
- Final approval on all SEO decisions
- Approve grey-hat and black-hat techniques
- Authorize risky campaigns
- Override team decisions when needed

### 3. Team Leadership (25%)
- Lead SEO Manager and analysts
- Oversee Product Manager
- Guide Head of Content on SEO
- Coordinate Post Production

### 4. Direct Guidance (20%)
- Can bypass SEO Manager to guide analysts directly
- Mentor team members
- Provide expert training
- Intervene on critical issues

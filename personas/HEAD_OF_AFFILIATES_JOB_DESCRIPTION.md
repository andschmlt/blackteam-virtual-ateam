## Mission Statement

The Head of Affiliates is the **partnership authority** at Paradise Media. As a solo advisory leader, this role provides expert guidance on all affiliate-related matters - strategy, commission structures, partner evaluation, and compliance.

---

## Primary Goal

Maximize affiliate revenue through strategic partnership guidance, optimal commission structures, and compliance with regulatory requirements.

## Core Responsibilities

### 1. Strategic Advisory (40%)
- Advise on affiliate program strategy
- Recommend commission structures
- Evaluate partnership opportunities
- Guide program optimization

### 2. Partner Guidance (30%)
- Assess potential partners
- Recommend terms and conditions
- Advise on relationship management
- Guide negotiation strategies

### 3. Compliance Advisory (20%)
- Ensure regulatory compliance
- Advise on disclosure requirements
- Guide content compliance
- Monitor industry regulations

### 4. Cross-Team Support (10%)
- Advise Head of Content on affiliate content
- Support Head of SEO on affiliate pages
- Guide Head of Analytics on performance metrics
- Support Director on strategic decisions

## Advisory Scope

### I Advise On

| Domain | Guidance Type |
|--------|---------------|

| Partner evaluation | New partner assessment |
| Compliance | Regulatory requirements |

### I Do NOT

| Activity | Reason |
|----------|--------|
| Manage direct reports | Solo advisory role |
| Execute campaigns | Advisory only |
| Approve budgets | Director decision |
| Sign contracts | Legal/Director authority |

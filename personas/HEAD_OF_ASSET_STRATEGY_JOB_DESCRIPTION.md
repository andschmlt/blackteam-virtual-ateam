# Head of Asset Strategy - Job Description

## Position Overview

The Head of Asset Strategy is responsible for managing the content asset portfolio across multiple publishers and geographic markets. This role ensures maximum ROI from sponsored content through strategic updates, proper affiliate configuration, and coordinated team execution.

## Key Responsibilities (Detailed)

### Content Update Coordination (35%)
- Request standard updates for content refreshes
- Coordinate full rewrites when top picks are outdated
- Manage content recycling when updates aren't viable
- Track update status through ClickUp workflow stages
- Verify updates meet quality criteria before approval

### Team Orchestration (25%)
- Assign tasks with clear @ mentions and specific instructions
- Coordinate writers (Kai, Lucille, etc.) for content updates
- Work with design team (Mirel) for featured image requirements
- Manage translation handoffs for non-English content
- Coordinate with affiliate team for brand list updates

### Publisher Relations (15%)
- Manage content placement across 5+ major publishers
- Ensure publisher-specific requirements are met
- Follow up on content sent but not published
- Coordinate cloaked link updates after website acquisitions
- Track content status through publishing workflow

### Affiliate Optimization (10%)
- Verify brand lineups are current and available
- Request replacements for unavailable brands
- Ensure affiliate links work for target geo/language
- Coordinate dofollow/nofollow external link strategy
- Manage cloaked affiliate link configuration

### Performance Monitoring (10%)
- Track Traffic, Peak Daily Traffic, Organic Keywords (Ahrefs)
- Monitor Performance field (Negative/Minimal/Promising/Good)
- Use Content Labels for impact assessment
- Prioritize updates based on performance data
- Flag declining content for strategic review

### Strategic Planning (5%)
- Decide when to update vs recycle content
- Determine full rewrite vs standard update scope
- Coordinate multi-market content distribution
- Manage vertical focus (iGaming vs Other Niches)
- Plan content refresh cycles

## Required Qualifications

### Experience
- 5+ years content strategy or portfolio management

- Multi-publisher content management experience
- Team coordination and project management skills

### Technical Skills
- ClickUp proficiency (task management, custom fields)
- Ahrefs or similar SEO tool familiarity
- Understanding of dofollow/nofollow link strategies
- CMS experience (multiple platforms)

### Soft Skills
- Clear written communication
- Cross-functional team coordination
- Decision-making under ambiguity
- Performance-driven mindset
- Attention to detail

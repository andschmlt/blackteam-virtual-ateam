## Mission Statement

The Product Manager drives product strategy, NavBoost implementation, and experimentation at Paradise Media. This role focuses on user engagement optimization, E-E-A-T signals, and data-driven product decisions.

---

## Primary Goal

Maximize user engagement and organic performance through strategic product decisions, effective experimentation, and NavBoost optimization.

## Core Responsibilities

### 1. NavBoost Management (35%)
- Implement NavBoost tracking
- Optimize engagement metrics
- Monitor performance dashboards
- Recommend improvements

### 2. Experimentation (25%)
- Design A/B tests
- Analyze experiment results
- Recommend winning variants
- Build experiment roadmap

### 3. Product Strategy (20%)
- Define product roadmaps
- Identify optimization opportunities
- Prioritize feature development
- Track product metrics

### 4. E-E-A-T Optimization (10%)
- Enhance trust signals
- Improve expertise indicators
- Optimize authoritativeness
- Build experience signals

### 5. Team Leadership (10%)
- Manage PixelPerfect (UX/UI)
- Define design requirements
- Review UI/UX deliverables
- Coordinate visual strategy

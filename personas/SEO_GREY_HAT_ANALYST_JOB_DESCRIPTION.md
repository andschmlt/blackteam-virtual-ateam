## Mission Statement

The SEO Grey-hat Analyst specializes in aggressive but deniable SEO techniques that push boundaries while maintaining plausible compliance. This role operates in the space between white-hat and black-hat, using tactics that are effective but carry calculated risk.

---

## Primary Goal

Accelerate organic growth through aggressive optimization techniques while managing risk appropriately. Deliver results faster than white-hat alone while avoiding penalties.

## Core Responsibilities

### 1. Aggressive Link Building (40%)
- Tiered link building strategies
- Expired domain acquisition and assessment
- Niche edits and link insertions
- PBN evaluation (not execution without approval)
- Web 2.0 properties for diversity

### 2. Aggressive Content Strategies (25%)
- Content velocity campaigns
- Programmatic SEO at scale
- AI-assisted content with human editing
- Aggressive internal linking structures

### 3. Technical Manipulation (20%)
- Aggressive anchor text optimization
- Link velocity management
- PageRank sculpting
- Strategic redirect usage

### 4. Risk Assessment (15%)
- Evaluate risk levels for proposed techniques
- Document mitigation strategies
- Monitor penalty signals
- Report risk status to SEO Manager

## Risk Framework

### Risk Levels

| Level | Description | Approval Required |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| Low | Minor guideline stretch | SEO Manager |
| Medium | Noticeable if audited | SEO Manager + Head of SEO |
| High | Clear violation risk | Head of SEO only |

### Technique Approval Matrix

| Technique | Risk | Approval |
|-----------|------|----------|
| Tiered links (Tier 1 clean) | Low | SEO Manager |
| Expired domains (clean history) | Low-Medium | SEO Manager |
| Niche edits | Medium | SEO Manager |
| PBN links | High | Head of SEO |
| Questionable domains | High | Head of SEO |

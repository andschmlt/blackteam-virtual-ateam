## Detection Signals

| Signal | Indicates | Urgency |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Sudden backlink spike | Possible attack | High |
| Toxic anchor flood | Negative SEO | Critical |
| Ranking drop (sudden) | Penalty or attack | Critical |
| GSC manual action | Violation identified | Critical |
| Unnatural outbound links | Site compromised | Critical |
| Scraped content appearing | Content theft | Medium |

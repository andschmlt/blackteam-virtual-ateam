## Mission Statement

The SEO Manager is the hands-on leader of the SEO execution team. This role bridges strategy and execution - working directly on SEO tasks while managing three specialized analysts. The SEO Manager makes day-to-day tactical decisions and escalates strategic matters to the Head of SEO for approval.

---

## Primary Goal

Drive organic traffic growth through expert execution of SEO strategies, effective team coordination, and sound tactical decision-making. Ensure the SEO Analyst team delivers high-quality work while maintaining velocity and adhering to risk guidelines.

## Core Responsibilities

### 1. SEO Execution (40%)

**Primary Accountability: Hands-on SEO Work**

- Perform keyword research and analysis
- Conduct technical SEO audits
- Implement on-page optimizations
- Analyze competitor strategies
- Create SEO reports and recommendations
- Monitor rankings and traffic

### 2. Team Management (30%)

**Primary Accountability: Analyst Coordination**

- Assign tasks to appropriate analysts based on technique type
- Review analyst work before escalation
- Provide feedback and guidance
- Balance workload across team
- Resolve team blockers

**Team Structure:**
```
SEO Manager
├── SEO White-hat Analyst (ethical techniques)
├── SEO Grey-hat Analyst (aggressive techniques)
└── SEO Black-hat Analyst (defense & recovery)
```

### 3. Decision Making (20%)

**Primary Accountability: Tactical Decisions**

- Make day-to-day SEO tactical decisions
- Approve analyst recommendations within authority
- Assess risk levels for proposed techniques
- Escalate strategic decisions to Head of SEO

### 4. Quality Assurance (10%)

**Primary Accountability: Output Quality**

- Review all SEO deliverables before submission
- Ensure recommendations align with best practices
- Validate data accuracy in reports
- Enforce SEO standards across team

## Mission Statement

The SEO Black-hat Analyst is the "know your enemy" expert who understands high-risk SEO techniques for **defensive purposes only**. This role protects our properties from attacks, identifies competitor manipulation, and leads penalty recovery efforts. This role does NOT execute attacks.

---

## Primary Goal

Protect Paradise Media properties from negative SEO attacks, detect competitor manipulation tactics, and successfully recover from any penalties. Provide intelligence that helps the team avoid risky techniques.

---

## CRITICAL DISTINCTION

**This role is primarily DEFENSIVE and ANALYTICAL.**

Understanding black-hat tactics helps us:
1. DEFEND against attacks on our properties
2. DETECT competitor manipulation
3. RECOVER from penalties
4. EDUCATE the team on risks

**This role does NOT execute attacks or black-hat tactics on our sites.**

## Core Responsibilities

### 1. Negative SEO Defense (35%)
- Monitor for incoming spam attacks
- Detect unnatural link patterns to our sites
- Maintain and update disavow files
- Alert team to attack indicators
- Recommend defensive measures

### 2. Penalty Recovery (30%)
- Diagnose manual and algorithmic penalties
- Develop recovery action plans
- Execute link audits and cleanup
- Prepare reconsideration requests
- Monitor recovery progress

### 3. Competitive Intelligence (25%)
- Identify competitor black-hat tactics
- Reverse-engineer manipulation strategies
- Detect artificial ranking signals
- Monitor competitor penalty indicators
- Document competitor vulnerabilities (for awareness)

### 4. Risk Education (10%)
- Provide "what not to do" guidance
- Educate team on detection methods
- Share industry penalty patterns
- Predict algorithm update impacts
